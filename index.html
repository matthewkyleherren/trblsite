More About Us

  • British Airways

    Vide Infra is an Approved Supplier for British Airways, the flag carrier airline of the United Kingdom, for digital product development globally. For many years, we've been delivering various customer self-care, BPM, customer loyalty, and customer portal solutions for the company worldwide. The geography of our projects for BA covers an area from Argentina to Japan.

  • Bite

    Bite, one of the largest MNO's in the Baltic states, approached us to streamline their customer-facing products. We significantly improved user experience and boost business performance for the marketing website, eСommerce store, and online self-care system.

  • airBaltic

    We've provided product development services to the flag-carrying airline of Latvia for several special initiatives, including upfront high-cuisine meals and mobile card ordering for the customers. We've secured full-cycle solutions, from UX design to development and maintenance.

  • DNB

    DNB is Norway's largest financial services group. We have developed numerous digital products for the company, including the group's corporate portal, a corporate portal for Latvia, and multiple other corporate and promo websites.

  • GE Money Bank

    GE Money Bank was a universal bank owned by GE, offering loan and deposit products to both private and legal entities. The Latvian branch of the company approached us to develop a custom dealer front-end BPM system that automated loan origination in the company's outlets and partner shops.

  • SEB

    We've created several vital online products for the Latvian branch of one of the largest Scandinavian financial groups, including the corporate website and the bank's corporate portal.

  • ABLV

    During our cooperation with ABLV, one of the largest private banks in the Baltic States, we've developed several large-scale digital products for the company, including a corporate portal and the main corporate website.
