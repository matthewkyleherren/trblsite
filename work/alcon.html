Alcon Group

Corporate Website

Visit Website

    Rating Runeta

Alcon Group is a real estate developer in Moscow with a focus on office building development and management. A full-cycle approach and following demanding quality standards set the company apart. Our goal was to create an impactful website capable of transmitting the company’s unique attributes and create a solid impression in the minds of potential clients and partners.

The design tone fluctuates between strict and formal to lively and vivid due to a generally solid business style used as a platform and large typography, bright colours and animated illustrations on top of it.

Besides the projects’ and the company’s description, the website features useful links for various client types.

The company description page, implemented as a long-read, features an array of various interactive blocks and creative transitions to keep the visitors' attention focused on the presentation.

A rich company’s projects presentation make all the valuable facts and benefits clear in an inspiring and visually attractive way.
