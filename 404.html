33

CLIENT

Jagwar Twin

PROJECT

Everything that came before each of us is already inside of us, encoded in our DNA waiting to be discovered. On the flip side, everything we do here on the planet alters our DNA and therefore impacts collective consciousness for generations to come. There are 33 degrees of awakening consciousness already in you. Reclaim your crown! As an artist, Jagwar Twin has often felt at war with the concept of curating his digital self and the seeming need to be an influencer in this ever-connected digital world we all exist in. Many of the songs on 33 play with this duality and we wanted the art direction to directly reference both the pull between the digital and analog worlds, but also the concept of digital curation.

The Webby Awards

PROJECT

Each year, the Webby's selects a studio to build a design system rooted in the energy of the previous year in digital design. The 26th Annual Webby Awards were the first year back in a live venue since the pandemic and we had the honor of being asked to concept and develop the creative direction based around the concept of 'Bangers Only'. What we created was a maximalist nod to the connective tissue between music, the internet, pop culture, and contemporary design that wrapped the award show in a blitz of energy. The campaign touched all aspects of the event, including the :60 intro video, digital marketing campaign, stage design, in-venue signage, and award winner motion graphics.
